Insurance Waterfall

When a trader's PnL goes negative, losses are covered in order. Your balance sheet is never exposed.

1
User Balance
Seize from losing trader's collateral (normal case). Example: User has $10K, loses $3K → balance becomes $7K.
2
Insurance Pool
If user balance insufficient, insurance fund covers the shortfall. Example: $500K insurance pool → covers $50K deficit.
3
Broker Stake (Optional)
Additional protection layer from broker capital. You decide how much to stake — or none at all.
4
Socialized Loss
Last resort: distributed across winning traders proportionally. Rare, but system fails gracefully without touching your reserves.

Your balance sheet stays clean. Settlement risk is contained within the system.
